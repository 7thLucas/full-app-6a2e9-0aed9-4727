The Problem

Crores of Items.
Zero Earners.

Indian homes own drills, cameras, projectors, and camping gear that sit unused for months at a time. Meanwhile, a neighbor pays full price to buy the same thing — or simply goes without. The peer-to-peer lending opportunity is largely untapped.

↓

The Solution

List It. Lend It. Earn.

A hyperlocal app that turns idle household items into income for lenders and affordable access for borrowers — for ₹20 to ₹100 per rental. Borrow → Use → Return. Simple.

List item → Borrow nearby → Return & rate
